Air Huarache

Born at the inventive hands of Tinker Hatfield, the Air Huarache redefined streamlined performance upon its debut in 1991 by asking a simple question: "Have you hugged your foot today?" Now, the all-time favourite is back in a new 'Toadstool' make-up. Toadstool-coloured leather and Chestnut Brown laces mix with a super-breathable, neoprene-like black fabric for easy styling. In true OG fashion, the silhouette's low-cut collar, iconic heel clip and bootie-like construction remain in place in familiar form.
